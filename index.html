Understanding AQI

0 – 50

Good

Air quality is satisfactory. Little or no risk to health.

51 – 100

Satisfactory

Acceptable air quality. Minor concern for sensitive groups.

101 – 200

Moderate

Sensitive individuals may experience health effects.

201 – 300

Poor

Health effects for everyone; serious effects for sensitive groups.

301 – 400

Very Poor

Health alert: everyone may experience serious effects.

401+

Severe

Emergency conditions. Entire population likely affected.
